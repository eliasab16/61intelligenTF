Welcome to Harvard's CS61 (barely) intelligenTF

I'm a simple prototype of a virtual TF (assistant)
for Harvard's CS61 course. I'm a contextual chatbot
that helps answer students' common requests, such as inquiring
about grading server errors, asking for extensions, etc.
In my current state, I'm not capable of engaging in all kinds of conversations with you.
If I get stuck in a conversation, please be patient with me
- and maybe start over - as I'm still learning.

Chat with intelligenTF below

You don't have to over-explain your request.
Try to keep messages and requests short, and I'll ask you for explanations in case I need any.
A greeting would be nice to start with ;) but you can also just cut straight to the point.
